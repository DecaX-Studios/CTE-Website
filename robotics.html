What Robotics Offers

Discover cutting-edge career and technical education programs designed to prepare you for tomorrow's opportunities

MS

Mechanical Systems

Students explore the principles of mechanics, motion, and machine design. Through hands-on projects, they learn how components like gears, motors, and sensors interact to create efficient robotic systems. The course emphasizes problem-solving, precision, and practical engineering foundations.

PA

Programming & Automation

In this course, students develop the logic and coding skills that bring robots to life. They use programming languages and control systems to automate motion, process data, and integrate feedback. The program nurtures analytical thinking and computational fluency essential to modern engineering.

ECS

Electronics & Control Systems

Students learn how electrical circuits, sensors, and microcontrollers power robotic devices. They design and test systems that respond to environmental input, focusing on accuracy, calibration, and system reliability. This course builds the bridge between hardware and intelligent automation.

EDST

Engineering Design & Systems Thinking

This category challenges students to think like engineers, breaking down problems, designing prototypes, and iterating toward optimal solutions. Emphasis is placed on teamwork, technical documentation, and design methodology that connects mechanical, electrical, and computational disciplines.

CR

Competitive Robotics

Students put their knowledge to the test in team-based robotics competitions. From design and coding to troubleshooting under pressure, they learn adaptability, collaboration, and leadership. Competitions build confidence and connect classroom learning to real-world application.

ISP

Industry Scholars Program

The Industry Scholars Program offers students professional experience within STEM and technology fields. Through partnerships with industry mentors and organizations, students apply robotics, coding, and engineering concepts in authentic settings, building both technical proficiency and career readiness.
