What Cybersecurity Offers

Discover cutting-edge career and technical education programs designed to prepare you for tomorrow's opportunities

NI

Network Infrastructure

Students learn the foundations of network design, communication protocols, and system connectivity. Through hands-on labs, they build and secure networks, understanding how data travels across hardware and the internet while developing problem-solving and diagnostic skills.

ISE

Information Security & Encryption

This category explores the principles of digital protection, from data encryption to authentication systems. Students analyze vulnerabilities, apply security measures, and learn ethical responsibility in safeguarding information against modern threats.

SA

Systems Administration

Students gain practical experience in managing computer systems and operating environments. They configure servers, manage databases, and maintain user access, developing the technical foundation needed for IT and cybersecurity operations.

PS

Programming & Scripting

This course focuses on automation and backend logic through languages like Python, C#, and Bash. Students write scripts to detect anomalies, streamline workflows, and reinforce system defenses, learning to think both like developers and defenders.

CDE

Cyber Defense & Ethics

Students simulate real-world cybersecurity scenarios, responding to breaches and managing digital crises in a controlled environment. They learn to approach security with a balance of strategy, precision, and integrity — preparing to protect systems, data, and users in the digital age.

ISP

Industry Scholars Program

The Industry Scholars Program connects students with cybersecurity professionals and real-world technical environments. Through guided internships and mentorship, students gain firsthand experience in system management, data protection, and IT operations, translating classroom learning into professional readiness.
