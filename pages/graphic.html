What Graphic Design Offers

Discover cutting-edge career and technical education programs designed to prepare you for tomorrow's opportunities

T

Typography

Students explore the art and science of type, how lettering, spacing, and hierarchy shape communication. From hand-drawn concepts to digital applications, typography teaches visual clarity, consistency, and creative precision essential for every designer.

VD

Visual Design

Visual Design introduces students to the foundations of composition, color theory, and branding. Through hands-on projects, students learn to balance aesthetics and function, crafting designs that communicate purpose and emotion across print and digital media.

DMD

Digital Media Design

In this course, students harness industry-standard software to create logos, layouts, and digital illustrations. They develop fluency in programs such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, while refining visual problem-solving skills and workflow efficiency.

CS

Creative Strategy

In this course, students learn to develop innovative concepts and strategies for visual communication. They explore the intersection of creativity and technology, gaining skills in brainstorming, ideation, and project development.

BI

Branding & Identity

Branding and Identity design trains students to build cohesive visual systems for businesses, events, and organizations. Students study logo design, style guides, and brand storytelling to create professional-level presentations that reflect both vision and strategy.

ISP

Industry Scholars Program

Gain hands-on experience in professional design environments through the Industry Scholars Program. Students collaborate with industry partners and mentors on real-world projects, applying classroom knowledge to meet client needs while refining technical and professional skills for future creative careers.

About the Leader

Mr. Hadel

Mr. Hadel leads the Graphic Design Academy, guiding students through the creative and technical dimensions of design. With many years of teaching experience and a strong foundation in digital media, typography, and branding, he helps students turn artistic vision into professional skill. His approach blends creativity with career readiness, inspiring students to think critically, design purposefully, and pursue excellence in every project.
