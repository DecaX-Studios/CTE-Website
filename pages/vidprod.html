What Film Production Offers

Discover cutting-edge career and technical education programs designed to prepare you for tomorrow's opportunities

A

Acting

Step into the spotlight and learn the art of performance from script to screen. Students develop confidence, emotional expression, and on-camera presence through scene work, improvisation, and storytelling. Acting cultivates communication, collaboration, and creative discipline, all essential skills for any future in film or theater.

D

Direction

Direction empowers students to transform vision into reality. Through hands-on experience in shot composition, storyboarding, and leadership on set, students learn to guide actors, crews, and creative projects with clarity and purpose. This emphasizes creative decision-making, collaboration, and the craft of visual storytelling.

P

Production

Behind every great film is a team that brings it to life. In production, students master the technical and organizational aspects of filmmaking. From camera operation and lighting to sound design and editing. The focus is on teamwork, professionalism, and the ability to adapt under real-world production conditions.

BHP

BlackHawks Productions

In the BHP internship, students take on real-world projects, producing content for school and community clients. This program emphasizes professional standards, client communication, and project management. Students gain valuable experience in a collaborative production environment, as well as work-based learning hours, which are crucial for building a professional portfolio and obtaining their industry certifications.

A

Animation

In Animation, students combine art and technology to craft visual stories frame by frame. They explore 2D and 3D animation, motion graphics, and digital design while learning industry-standard software. The program emphasizes creativity, patience, and technical skill, preparing students to turn imagination into motion.

ISP

Industry Scholars Program

Gain hands-on experience in a professional film media environment through our structured internship program. Students are placed with industry‐aligned partners where they apply classroom skills in real settings, working alongside mentors in tasks ranging from pre-production through post-production. This program elevates students’ technical competencies, builds their professional networks, and enhances their readiness for both further study and careers in film and media.
